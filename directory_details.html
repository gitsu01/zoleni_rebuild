Public Storage is one of the largest self-storage companies in the world,
with a significant presence in the United States, Europe, and Canada.

  • Company Overview

    Public Storage was founded in 1972 and is headquartered in Glendale, California, USA. It operates thousands of self-storage facilities, making it one of the largest real estate investment trusts (REITs) specializing in self-storage.

  • Global Presence

    Public Storage has a widespread network of self-storage facilities across the United States, with locations in all 50 states. Additionally, it has expanded its operations internationally, with facilities in Europe (primarily in the United Kingdom) and Canada.

  • Storage Unit Options

    Public Storage offers a variety of storage unit sizes to accommodate different storage needs, ranging from small locker-sized units to large warehouse-style spaces. Many facilities also provide climate-controlled units to protect temperature-sensitive items.

  • Security Measures

    Public Storage prioritizes the security of customers' belongings and implements various security measures across its facilities. These may include gated access with keypad entry, surveillance cameras, individually alarmed units, and onsite management or security personnel.

  • Convenience and Accessibility

    Public Storage aims to provide convenient access to customers' stored belongings. Facilities typically offer extended access hours, allowing renters to access their units outside of regular business hours. Some locations may even provide 24/7 access for added convenience.

  • Online Services

    Public Storage offers online account management tools that allow renters to manage their storage units remotely. Through an online portal or mobile app, customers can make payments, view account information, and reserve or upgrade units without visiting the facility in person.

  • Customer Service

    Public Storage places a strong emphasis on customer service and strives to provide a positive experience for its renters. The company's website and customer service representatives are available to assist customers with inquiries, reservations, and other needs.

  • Overall, Public Storage is a prominent player in the self-storage industry, known for its extensive network of facilities, security measures, and customer-friendly services. Its widespread presence and range of storage options make it a popular choice for individuals and businesses seeking storage solutions.
